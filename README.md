# AI Internship Simulator

### Practicing real internship tasks to bridge the gap between learning and experience

## Overview

AI Internship Simulator is a platform designed to help students gain real-world internship experience before applying to companies.

Many students face rejection during internship applications because they lack practical experience. Our platform solves this problem by simulating real internship environments where students can practice industry-style tasks and receive AI-based feedback.

This allows students to build practical skills, improve their portfolios, and become industry-ready.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to secure internships due to:

* Lack of real-world experience
* College education being mostly theory-based
* Companies expecting practical project skills

This creates a major gap between **academic learning and industry expectations**.

---

## Limitations of Current Solutions

Existing platforms such as online courses and tutorials:

* Do not simulate real company workflows
* Provide limited practical experience
* Lack structured performance evaluation

Students still graduate without knowing how real internship work actually happens.

---

## Our Solution

AI Internship Simulator provides a **virtual internship environment powered by AI**.

Students can:

* Choose a domain
* Work on simulated internship tasks
* Submit projects
* Receive AI-based feedback and performance reports

This creates a safe environment where students can practice before applying for real internships.

---

## How It Works

1. Student registers on the platform
2. Student selects a domain (Web Dev, Data Science, etc.)
3. AI generates internship-style tasks and projects
4. Student submits work
5. AI analyzes submissions and provides feedback
6. Student receives a performance report and skill insights
